# User Research

## Problem Statement

Many people struggle to understand and manage their emotions. Due to busy schedules and daily stress, users often forget to reflect on their emotional well-being.

## Research Findings

- Users prefer a simple and clean interface.
- Users want quick mood tracking with minimal effort.
- Users appreciate daily reminders.
- Users want to review past emotions and patterns.

## User Needs

- One-tap mood selection
- Daily emotional tracking
- Easy access to mood history
- Simple and intuitive navigation
